About Me

With over a decade of experience in the design industry, I specialize in creating visual identities that communicate clearly and resonate deeply. My approach combines strategic thinking with meticulous craftsmanship to deliver solutions that are both beautiful and effective.

After studying Visual Communication at the Rhode Island School of Design, I worked with leading agencies in New York and London before establishing my independent practice in 2018.

When I'm not designing, you can find me hiking in the mountains or collecting vintage design annuals.
